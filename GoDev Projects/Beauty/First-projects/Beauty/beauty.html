HydraFacial

The latest non-invasive skin resurfacing treatment, ideal for treating fine lines and wrinkles, hyperpigmentation, oily and acne-prone skin, as well as general skin rejuvenation.

The ultimate medical facial

When you entrust something as precious as your face to someone, you want to know that you're in the best and safest hands.The UK's leading cosmetic surgeon, has handpicked the best aesthetic team in the business to look after you.

HydraFacial is the latest non-invasive skin resurfacing treatment. It combines deep cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, hydration (hyaluronic acid) and powerful antioxidant protection, as well as using red LED light in the final stage to stimulate collagen and elastin production. For the optimum results, we recommend a course of one treatment per week for six weeks, but after only one treatment you will notice that your skin feels firm, plump and that people comment on your healthy glow.

This treatment is a favourite with celebrities and skin care professionals because it is soothing, nourishing, non-invasive and gives instant results. This is the perfect choice if you want to look for your best for a special occasion or red carpet event. How does it work?

HydraFacial is a non-invasive skin resurfacing and rejuvenating treatment, which combines cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, hydration and antioxidant protection. It is less abrasive than microdermabrasion and delivers significantly better results. It's also soothing, moisturising and non-invasive.

During the treatment we use a special hand piece to remove dead skin cells and impurities in the skin, whilst infusing it with cleansing, hydrating, and antioxidant serums. We finish by shining red LED lights on to the skin to boost its circulation, reduce redness and irritation, and to stimulate collagen and elastin production.

HydraFacial gives instant results and leaves you with hydrated skin and a more youthful appearance. You can apply make-up right after the treatment and you won't need to do anything special afterwards or take time off work.

What happens during a treatment?

During your HydraFacial treatment, we will use a series of special tools, called HydroPeel tips, to remove dead skin cells and impurities in your skin, while infusing it with cleansing, hydrating, and moisturising serums.

Each HydroPeel tip has multiple edges that exfoliate the skin. The tip is designed to create a vortex effect, which easily dislodges and removes impurities in the skin, while simultaneously treating it with hydrating serums and antioxidants. We pass the tip over your skin several times to get even results.

We'll begin the treatment with cleansing and exfoliation to remove dead skin cells and reveal the healthy new skin beneath. A painless vacuum extraction removes impurities from your pores.

We can also use a glycolic and salicylic acid peel, depending on your skin condition, but we would discuss this with you in advance if we feel this should be part of your unique treatment plan.

The final stage is hydration. We'll infuse your skin with antioxidants and hyaluronic acid (a natural substance found in the body) to hydrate, nourish and protect it. We blend the treatment serums specifically for your skin condition.

What can HidraFacial be used to treat?

HydraFacial is ideal for treating fine lines and wrinkles, hyperpigmentation and oily and acne-prone skin, as well as general rejuvenation of the skin.
